01 Our Mission

Education that forms the whole student.

TACA exists to provide an affordable, Christ-centered education that enriches the lives of students and families through Biblical principles, academic excellence, athletics, fine arts, and outreach ministry.

We believe a child's mind is best developed when nurtured alongside the heart and the soul — together, not separately.

From kindergarten through senior year, our students grow spiritually, intellectually, emotionally, and socially. Every student receives Biblical instruction daily and gathers for Chapel each Wednesday — a rhythm that has shaped this community since 1994.

03 · Student Life

Beyond the classroom.

A TACA education extends well past textbooks. From Crusader athletics to drama and praise band, from chapel to community service — students discover their gifts and learn to use them in service to others.

No. 01

Crusader Athletics

Volleyball, basketball, cross country, cheer, and a partnership with Bristol Soccer Association. Competition begins in 5th grade — but character building begins on day one.

Volleyball Basketball Cross Country Cheer Soccer
No. 02

Fine Arts

Art, drama, and praise & worship — students lift their voices and shape their craft from elementary through high school.
